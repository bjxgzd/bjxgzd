Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response after contacting you?

We guarantee a response within 24 business hours for all inquiries. For urgent matters during business hours, you can expect a response within 2-4 hours. Our team prioritizes timely communication to ensure your project moves forward efficiently.

What information should I prepare before our first consultation?

To make our initial consultation as productive as possible, please prepare: your project goals and objectives, target audience information, competitor analysis (if available), timeline expectations, budget range, and any specific features or requirements you have in mind. Don't worry if you don't have everything ready - we can help you refine your ideas during our discussion.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience working with clients across North America, Europe, Asia, and other regions. Our team is fluent in English and Chinese, and we understand the cultural and market differences that are crucial for successful international projects. We use modern collaboration tools to ensure seamless communication regardless of location.

What is your typical project timeline and process?

Our process begins with a discovery phase (1-2 weeks), followed by design and planning (2-4 weeks), development (2-6 months depending on complexity), testing and quality assurance (2-4 weeks), and launch with post-launch support. Simple apps can be completed in 2-3 months, while complex projects with backend systems and game development may take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines and milestones during the initial consultation.

How do you handle intellectual property and confidentiality?

We take intellectual property and confidentiality very seriously. All our engagements include comprehensive non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) and clear intellectual property ownership terms. Your ideas, code, designs, and business information remain your property, and we implement strict security protocols to protect your data throughout the development process.

What payment methods and terms do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards. Our typical payment structure includes an initial deposit (20-30%), milestone payments based on project progress, and a final payment upon completion and delivery. For long-term partnerships, we can also arrange monthly retainer agreements. All payment terms are clearly outlined in our contracts.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support packages including bug fixes, performance monitoring, feature updates, security patches, and platform compatibility updates. We understand that successful digital products require ongoing attention, and we're committed to supporting our clients' long-term success. Support packages can be customized based on your specific needs and budget.
